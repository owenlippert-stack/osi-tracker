About this reading. The OSI is an analytical construct built by Opposition International from a subset of V-Dem indicators (opposition autonomy, accountability, party cohesion, electoral integrity, civil liberties). It is not an official V-Dem index. Scores are annual; the latest reflect the situation on 31 Dec 2025. Election sub-scores carry forward from the last coded election, so they can lag a recent transition.
Source data: V-Dem Country-Year Dataset v16 (Coppedge et al., Varieties of Democracy Project, 2026), used under CC-BY.
